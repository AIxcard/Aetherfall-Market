Aetherfall Chronicle

In the ancient world of Aetherfall, power was not mined from the earth or drawn from the sky. It was created through effort. Every strike, every clash, every movement of will caused the fragments of the world to resonate. Warriors learned that repeated action could awaken something hidden inside the universe itself.

Over centuries, the fragments grew unstable. Entire regions collapsed under the pressure of uncontrolled resonance. The ancient orders vanished, leaving behind only scattered shards and forgotten teachings.

Now a new wielder rises to rediscover the lost balance between effort and power.
