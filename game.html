Nexus Guide

Welcome to the Aetherfall Terminal. Clicks have a 1% chance for Gold and 0.8% for Fragments. Critical hits (10% chance) double your click value. Your damage scales with your Combo: for every 1,500 hits without stopping, you gain +1 Click to your base power. Chapter progression is linear. Bronze Coupons offer 5 Baht off, Silver 10 Baht, and Gold 25 Baht.
