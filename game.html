Nexus Protocol: Guide

1. POWER SCALING: Your base power increases through the Forge (Gold) and Fragment Shop (Fragments). Every 1,500 Combo hits adds +1 temporary Power.

2. CRITICAL HITS: There is a 10% chance to trigger a Mega-Crit, dealing 2x Damage and providing a massive visual burst.

3. STORY MODE: Each story part requires a set number of hits. IMPORTANT: When you claim a story reward, your "Current Progress" resets to 0 for the next part.

4. LEVELING: Max level is 100. If you have excess EXP, you will level up 1 level per click until the buffer is clear.

5. COUPONS: Coupons have limited stock and restock every 7 days. Check the timers in the sidebar.
