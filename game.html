Lore

The Aetherfall blade was discovered deep within an abandoned facility. Each strike extracts unstable energy fragments from its core. Researchers once tried to stabilize the blade’s power — but failed. Now the blade responds only to repeated strikes. The more energy extracted, the stronger the rewards become.

Guide

Click the blade to gain energy. Energy converts into clicks and coins. Coins unlock upgrades and coupons. Fragments unlock rare upgrades. Maintain combos by clicking continuously.
