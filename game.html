Lore

Fragments were discovered beneath the ruins of an abandoned research complex. Scientists quickly realized these crystalline shards reacted violently when struck repeatedly.

Every strike amplified their resonance output. Eventually engineers built entire power systems around fragment resonance technology.

But fragments were not native to Earth. They came from microscopic dimensional fractures beneath the crust.

When those fractures widened, the Resonance Collapse began. Cities powered by fragments destabilized overnight.

Now fragments are rare and traded through underground markets like the Abyssal Exchange.

Guide

Click the sword to generate resonance energy. Each click increases your total clicks and contributes to your combo.

Combos increase your click power. If you stop clicking for more than 3 seconds, the combo resets.

Coins are earned through missions, random drops, and milestones. Use them to purchase upgrades in the shop.

Fragments are rare resources used for powerful permanent upgrades.

Milestones unlock every 5 levels up to level 100 and provide large rewards.
