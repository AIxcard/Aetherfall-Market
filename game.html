NEXUS DATA-STREAM

CORE MECHANICS: Every click harvests Aether. There is a 1% base chance to find Gold and a 0.8% chance for Void Fragments.

COMBO SYSTEM: Maintaining a steady rhythm builds your Combo. Every 1,500 hits grants +1 Base Power. Beware: the combo expires if you stop clicking for more than 1.8 seconds.

CRITICAL SURGES: You have a 10% chance to trigger a Critical Strike. This doubles your click power and provides a massive boost to EXP gain. Achievements can further enhance these multipliers.

PROGRESSION: Use the Forge for Gold-based upgrades and the Fragment Shop for rare power boosts. Coupons in the market provide massive late-game multipliers but are limited in stock.
