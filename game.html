Nexus Guide

CLICKS: Every click has a 1% chance to drop Gold and a 0.8% chance to drop Aether Fragments.

CRITICALS: You have a base 10% chance to deal 2x damage. This provides massive EXP boosts.

COMBO: Keep clicking! For every 1,500 combo, you gain +1 Click Power temporarily. If the timer runs out, the bonus disappears.

COUPONS: These are limited items that refresh. Keep them in your inventory for milestones or potential trade-ins.
